Core Framework

The most robust MEDDPICC model possible

At ConnectED, MEDDPICC isn't just a qualification checklist — it's a full sales conversation methodology. We embed every element into how your team thinks, asks, and sells. Our sharpest focus: Implicate the Pain.

M
Metrics
Strong & Measurable
We coach teams to uncover the quantifiable business impact that makes the case for change undeniable. Numbers close deals — vague value does not.
E
Economic Buyer
Find the Power
Who truly controls the budget and can say yes? We build the skills to identify, access, and influence the real decision-maker — early.
D
Decision Criteria
Know the Scorecard
The technical, commercial, and personal criteria on which you are being evaluated — understood, shaped, and aligned to your strengths.
D
Decision Process
Map the Journey
Every step, every stakeholder, every internal gate — mapped and navigated proactively so nothing surprises you in the final stretch.
P
Paper Process
Own the Close
Legal, procurement, and signature can kill deals late. We train sellers to qualify and actively manage the paper process from day one of the opportunity.
I
Implicate the Pain
The Heart of It All
Our deepest focus. We develop conversations that uncover and implicate pain — making the cost of inaction feel real, personal, and urgent to the buyer.
C
Champion
Build Your Ally
A true champion has power, understands your value, and actively sells for you internally. We teach teams to build and sustain them throughout the deal.
C
Competition
Win the Contrast
We build the competitive intelligence discipline and positioning confidence to ensure the comparison between you and alternatives always works in your favour.

Sales Methodologies

Proven frameworks. Applied with precision.

SPIN Selling
Certified SPIN® Training Specialists. We build the questioning discipline that transforms sales conversations from pitches into genuine consultative dialogue — uncovering Situation, Problem, Implication, and Need-Payoff with precision.
Sandler Sales System
Proficient in the Sandler Sales Methodology. We apply the psychology of the buyer-seller dynamic to help sellers qualify hard, disqualify early, and close without pressure — operating from a position of confidence and control.
Command of the Message
Ensuring your sellers can articulate your value proposition with precision, consistency, and relevance to any buyer — in any situation, at any stage of the deal. Clarity creates momentum.
Challenger Sale
We develop sellers who teach, tailor, and take control — reframing a customer's thinking and creating the constructive tension that moves deals forward and differentiates you from the competition.
